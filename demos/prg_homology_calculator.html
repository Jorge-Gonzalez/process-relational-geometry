Single-object category: all generators are endomorphisms of one state *. The quotient category is a monoid; if inverses are declared, a group. Homology = H*(BG; ℤ), the homology of the classifying space.
Space-separated. Inverses are named a_inv, b_inv, etc. and are added automatically.

Each law l = r generates the congruence relation l ~ r. Laws are oriented as rewrite rules l→r (or r→l) by the shortlex term order. Confluence of the resulting TRS is checked automatically.
Determines how many morphism classes are enumerated (all words of length ≤ d in normal form). The computation is exact when the group is finite and depth ≥ group exponent. For infinite groups this is a finite approximation; stabilization is checked automatically.
